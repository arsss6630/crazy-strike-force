Crazy Strike Force is an intense first-person shooter game that combines fast-paced gunplay with strategic team-based combat. Customize your character with unique armors and skills, then battle against players from around the world in various game modes.

How to Play

WASD or Arrow Keys - Move your character

Left Click - Fire weapon

Right Click - Aim down sights

Space - Jump

Shift - Sprint

Q - Slide while running

C - Crouch

1, 2, 3 - Switch weapons

E / F - Interact / Pick up weapon

G - Throw grenade

H - Use medkit

Tab - Show scoreboard

Tips for Beginners

Start with Team Deathmatch to learn the maps and get comfortable with the controls. The M4A1 is a great all-around weapon for beginners. Always keep moving to avoid being an easy target, and use cover strategically.

As you progress, experiment with different perks to find your playstyle. Silent Walker is excellent for flanking, while Quickdraw Ammo helps in direct confrontations.
